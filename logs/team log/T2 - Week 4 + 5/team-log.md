## Reflection
- Lots of the infrastructures for the GUI are revamped, looked into, added functionality, and made available
- Interestingly, as we are fleshing out the GUI, we noticed that there is more and more functionality that needs to be added, to make the application competitive, and this is without regard for the GUI - this is purely functional
- The hypothesis is that it is easier to pinpoint the needs after the "done" is offered; we can benchmark on what a user would be seeing and would deem as acceptable - so our top priority is to reflect on this week's progress, and build upon that
- Speculation of issues within the next week is to fill out the GUI graphical + functional components, standardize the functionality of different GUI components (via this week's meeting), and possibly update our back end to facilitate the release of the app, and for the full implementation of the GUI
