# Finalized GUI Proposition

This is a composed and remastered version of everyone's proposition of the GUI design for Capstone Team 10.

The design is finalized based on:
1. Abiding by the design philosophy of our employer, UBC, with their color schemes, icons and all rights reserved.
2. Cost of manpower and "intergratability" (you know the saying of an architect dream's is an engineer's nightmare)
3. All of the features and aesthetic choices of the previous proposition made by the other teammates.
